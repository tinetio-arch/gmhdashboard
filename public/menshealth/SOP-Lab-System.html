üî¨ Ordering Labs

Creating a New Lab Order

  1. 1Go to Order Labs tab
  2. 2Click + Order Lab button
  3. 3Select the Patient from search
  4. 4Choose the appropriate Test Panel:
    • 9757 - Male Pre-Required Panel
    • 9761 - Male Post Panel
    • 9765 - Female Pre-Required Panel
    • 9760 - Female Post Panel
    • 146 - PSA Only
  5. 5Review patient demographics and submit

  2. 2Check the Pending Review count
